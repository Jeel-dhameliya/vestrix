Frequently Asked Questions

How can I schedule a personal styling consultation?

We offer complimentary personal styling consultations both in-store and virtually. Simply contact us through any of our channels, and our team will arrange a time that works perfectly for you.

Do you offer custom tailoring services?

Yes, our master tailors can create bespoke pieces tailored to your exact measurements and preferences. This service is available for select items and requires an initial consultation.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items with original tags attached. For exchanges, we provide complimentary shipping and will work with you to find the perfect alternative.

How do I care for my Vestrix garments?

Each garment comes with detailed care instructions. We recommend professional cleaning for most pieces and offer a complimentary care consultation with every purchase.
